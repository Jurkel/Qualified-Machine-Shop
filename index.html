The FogBuster

A non-fogging, low-pressure coolant delivery system that redefines shop safety.

Built specifically for CNC machines, lathes, and manual machining, the FogBuster is our own patented innovation. It delivers high-volume coolant exactly where you need it without the harmful mist and aerosol that traditionally pollutes shop air.

  • Healthier shop environment (no fog/mist)
  • Operates on low 10–20 PSI
  • Compatible with most liquid coolants
  • Precision instant on/off control
